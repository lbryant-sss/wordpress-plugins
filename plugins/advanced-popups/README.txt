== Description ==

Advanced Popups is a lightweight yet powerful WordPress popup plugin designed for maximum conversion. Create beautiful, targeted popups to grow your email list, display important announcements, show cookie notices, or promote special offers.

**Key Features:**

* **Multiple Popup Types** - Choose between Content Box (perfect for opt-ins), Notification Box (ideal for announcements), or Notification Bar (great for cookie notices)
* **Strategic Placements** - Position your popups where they'll perform best - top, bottom, center, corners, or sides of the screen
* **Smart Triggers** - Display popups at the perfect moment with multiple trigger options:
  * Time delay
  * Page viewed
  * Exit intent (catch visitors before they leave)
  * Scroll position (target engaged readers)
  * Manual launch via button click
  * Accept agreement (for compliance notices)
* **Precise Targeting** - Show popups exactly where they matter:
  * Specific pages, posts, or custom post types
  * Taxonomy pages (categories, tags)
  * Front page, archives, or search results
  * Based on user login status (guest vs. logged-in)
* **Visitor-Friendly Controls** - Respect your visitors with:
  * Frequency limits (avoid popup fatigue)
  * Mobile-specific settings
  * Multiple close options (overlay click, ESC key)
* **Beautiful Animations** - Choose from fade, slide, zoom, or combined effects for entrance and exit
* **Fully Customizable** - Control width, overlay appearance, and many other visual aspects

Advanced Popups is optimized for performance, ensuring your site speed isn't compromised while delivering high-converting popups that respect user experience.

Perfect for marketers, content creators, and site owners who want to increase engagement without annoying visitors.

**See Advanced Popups in action:** Check out our [live examples and demos](https://authentictheme.com/authentic/popups/) to see what you can create with this plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `advanced-popups` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings->Advanced Popups screen to configure the plugin.
4. Create your first popup under the 'Popups' menu.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I create a popup? =

After activating the plugin, you will see a new menu item called 'Popups' in your WordPress admin. Click on it and then click 'Add New' to create your first popup.

= Can I control where the popup appears? =

Yes, Advanced Popups offers precise targeting options. You can choose to display popups on specific pages, posts, or custom post types. You can also target taxonomy pages, front page, archives, or search results. Additionally, you can show popups based on the user's login status.

= How can I trigger the popup? =

The plugin provides multiple trigger options including time delay, page viewed, exit intent, scroll position, manual launch via button click, and accept agreement for compliance notices.

= Is it possible to limit how often a visitor sees the popup? =

Absolutely. Advanced Popups includes frequency limits to help avoid popup fatigue among your visitors.

= Are the popups mobile-friendly? =

Yes, the plugin includes mobile-specific settings to ensure that your popups look great and function well on all devices.

= What kind of animations can I use for the popups? =

You can choose from a variety of animations for both the entrance and exit of the popups, including fade, slide, zoom, or combined effects.

= Can I customize the appearance of the popups? =

Yes, Advanced Popups is fully customizable. You can control the width, overlay appearance, and many other visual aspects of the popups.

= Will this plugin slow down my website? =

No, Advanced Popups is optimized for performance, ensuring that your site speed isn't compromised while using the plugin.

= Who can benefit from using Advanced Popups? =

The plugin is perfect for marketers, content creators, and site owners who want to increase engagement on their website without annoying their visitors.
